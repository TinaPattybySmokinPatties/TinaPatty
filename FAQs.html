FAQs

Got questions? We've got answers.

How can I order online?

- Go to Menu and select which patty you would like to purchase, then click the cart, fill out your information, and select a mode of payment, then click Confirm Order.

How many days will be the estimated time for delivery of my order?

- Standard time of delivery upon ordering is 3-5 days since the products you will order are from the Philippines and might take a while.

Do you have a minimum order requirement?

- No, there is no minimum number of orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

- We accept Cash on Delivery, E-wallet, Debit/Credit Card.

Who are your partner courier/delivery company?

- Our courier are Flash Express TH and KEX Express Thailand.
